Code Addict

My passion is learning and improving. Coding provides me with the perfect vehicle for that expression of myself. There is always so much more to learn, and so many things to improve upon. I am a Front End Developer with a love for developing user interfaces and finding solutions to problems. I can build a small or large scale responsive site from the ground up as well as provide animations ranging from simple yet engaging UI enhancements to attention generating center pieces. I take a Mobile-First approach to everything I do and work hard to provide a great user experience on mobile devices as well as larger displays.

Fitness Freak

Fitness and nutrition provide another way for me to push myself and I do so daily. It is a huge part of who I am. It helps to keep me disciplined and focused as well to keep my body and mind agile. I bring my high level of energy and inexhaustible endurance into everything I do.

Dog Lover

I love all animals, but dogs have a special place in my heart. I am lucky enough to have two amazing dogs - Arwen and Moya. I love them with all my heart.

Musician

More
